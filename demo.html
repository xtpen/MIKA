MICA 101: A Beginner's Guide to Our Chatbot

Welcome to our Beginner's Guide to MICA! Here you'll discover everything you need to know to navigate and maximize your experience with MICA, our Medical Information Chatbot Assistant.

LET'S GET STARTED

LET'S GET STARTED!

ACCESS THE CHAT PAGE

You can start interacting with MICA by clicking the "Start Chatting" button on the homepage.

COMPOSE YOUR MESSAGE

Type your message or query into the chat input box. You can ask questions on illnesses, symptoms, or advice on wellness and general well-being.

SEND YOUR MESSAGE

After composing your message, press the "Send" button (illustrated as paper plane) or hit Enter button to submit your message to MICA.

EXPERIENCE A BONUS FEATURE!

SPEECH-TO-TEXT MESSAGE!

MICA is perfectly capable of processing voice messages and providing concise and accurate responses based on the user’s voice query.

HOW DO I "TALK" TO MICA?

USE THE SPEECH-TO-TEXT FEATURE

To use the Speech-to-Text feature, simply click the “Mic” button once then speak clearly your message to MICA. A few seconds after you finish speaking, the speech is then converted to text and sent to MICA.
